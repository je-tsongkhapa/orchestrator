# REFERENCE — Superorganism (SO) Business-Systems Map

The **superorganism preset** of the **Console** archetype — the user's life run *as an organization*, managed via the SO dashboard (a Console; sibling to ServiceGrid). Same Console machinery, morphed to the *human-life* domain. Sibling to [`REFERENCE-business-systems-map.md`](./REFERENCE-business-systems-map.md) (the FSM preset).

**47 systems** cut by the same 6 resources as the FSM map. Each row: what it manages · an analogous tool · build verdict (🟢 build / 🟡 embed / 🔴 skip) · build-state in the SO dashboard (✅/🟡/⚪/❌) · strategic fit.


## Money (10)

| System | Manages | Analog | Verdict | State | Strategic fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Portfolio & Position Ledger** | Every owned position across asset classes (stocks, crypto, metals, coins, land, liquid) with cost basis, quantity, current value, and target weight. | Kubera / Sharesight / Empower (Personal Capital) | 🟢 build | ✅ built | The asset-side record of the superorganism's balance sheet — the substrate every other money system reads from (allocation, net worth, tax, performance). Owning the ledger keeps it asset-class-agnostic in a way no consumer app supports: physical coins + land + crypto + equities under one finance_position/finance_opportunity schema. |
| **Capital Allocation & Opportunity Pipeline** | The deal/decision flow for deploying capital and time — opportunities staged explore→diligence→allocate→operate→rebalance→exit, each with a thesis and a 5-axis (financial/operational/knowledge/social/optionality) leverage profile. | Affinity / a VC deal-flow CRM repurposed personally | 🟢 build | ✅ built | The SO's signature differentiator: running personal capital as a portfolio of theses against a leverage-velocity objective, not a passive net-worth tracker. It is the orient+decide engine for money and the heart of the CapitalOS port (finance_opportunity is the unified money\|time spine). |
| **Allocation Policy / Strategy Mixer** | Target portfolio posture — 8 global preference faders (growth vs income, liquidity, volatility tolerance, inflation hedge, tax efficiency, simplicity, conviction vs diversification, time-to-compounding) plus 6 per-asset-class workbench knob sets, yielding a Fit Score. | Betterment/Wealthfront risk profile + an IPS / target-allocation model | 🟢 build | ✅ built | Encodes the investment policy statement as a tunable control surface rather than a static doc, making rebalance/reallocate suggestions derivable. It is the 'desired state' half that the rebalancing and review systems diff the ledger against (finance_mixer + finance_workbench + fitScore). |
| **Liabilities & Net Worth** | The debt side (mortgages, loans, credit) plus the assets−liabilities rollup and historical net-worth snapshots with component breakdown. | Empower / Monarch / Kubera net-worth dashboard | 🟢 build | 🟡 partial | The single headline number for financial health and the cross-domain metric the Metrics and Model domains want. The asset half is already the Position Ledger; the unbuilt half is liabilities — there is no debt table in the finance schema, and net_worth/savings_rate exist only as placeholder Metrics rows, so the true balance sheet is incomplete. |
| **Account & External-Platform Aggregation** | The roster of where money lives (brokerages, banks, wallets, retirement, entities) and the pipes that pull balances/transactions in automatically. | Plaid / Teller / MX (the aggregation layer) | 🟡 embed | 🟡 partial | Auto-aggregation is undifferentiated plumbing best embedded via Plaid/Teller and feeds the ledger + cashflow so they stay current without manual entry. | **Cash Flow & Budgeting** | Income and expense tracking, auto+manual categorization, per-category budgets, recurring commitments (subscriptions/memberships/gym/supplements with keep-cancel decisions), savings rate, and cash-flow forecasting. | Monarch Money / YNAB / Copilot (+ Rocket Money for the recurring slice) | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Closes the loop between the allocation engine (where capital should go) and operating reality (what comes in and goes out), and is the source of the savings rate that funds every opportunity. | **Income & Earning-Engine Management** | The portfolio of income streams (salary, business/SG income, dividends, yield) modeled as streams — their size, stability, growth, and concentration risk. | A personal P&L / income-diversification tracker (Tiller) | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Income is the inflow that powers allocation and is itself a portfolio to be diversified and de-risked; modeling streams (not just lump transactions) connects ServiceGrid business income to personal capital and feeds the time-opportunity side of the pipeline. 'income' appears only as mixer fader labels today — not built as a distinct system. |
| **Tax & Governance** | Tax profiles by entity/jurisdiction, estimated liability, tax-loss-harvesting opportunities, charitable giving, and document organization. | TurboTax / a tax-loss-harvesting service / a CPA workpaper | 🟡 embed | 🟡 partial | Compliance and filing logic is jurisdiction-heavy and best embedded/outsourced, but the SO owns the governance-pack shells that tag opportunities with the right entity/tax context. finance_governance_pack (entity_type, jurisdiction, tax_profile, compliance_checklist) exists as the container; actual harvesting/liability computation is not built. |
| **Performance & Review Rituals** | The backward-looking observe/calibrate layer: returns vs benchmarks, leverage-velocity history, milestones, and the cadenced rituals (monthly close, quarterly/annual review) that re-orient the whole money system. | Sharesight performance reports + a quarterly-review template | 🟢 build | 🟡 partial | Turns raw ledger movement into a verdict on whether the superorganism is on-track and drives the next reallocation. Review.tsx, History.tsx, finance_velocity_snapshot and finance_portfolio_snapshot exist; the IPS-anchored recurring-ritual scaffolding is only partly realized. |
| **Forecasting & Scenario Planning** | The forward-looking model: runway / FIRE number, retirement and time-to-financial-independence projections, and what-if scenarios over savings rate, returns, and allocation. | ProjectionLab / cFIREsim / Boldin (NewRetirement) | 🟢 build | ⚪ stub | The decide-ahead complement to backward-looking Review: it tells the superorganism whether the current trajectory reaches the target and which lever (savings rate, allocation, income) closes the gap. finance_goal (singleton velocity goal) is the only forward artifact and is leverage-velocity, not a money/time-to-FI model — so projection is effectively a stub. |

## People (8)

| System | Manages | Analog | Verdict | State | Strategic fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Relationship Graph (Personal CRM)** | The canonical registry of every human in your life as a primary entity — identity, how you're connected, relationship type/closeness, typed role overlays (mentor/peer/collaborator), and the durable per-person interaction history that records every meaningful touch, note, and commitment. | Monica / Clay / Dex (personal CRM); Reflect people-graph | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | People are the superorganism's highest-leverage external resource — capital, opportunities, and knowledge all flow through humans. The dashboard already has a Network topology engine (graphology + React Flow) and a Roadmap entity model, but no person registry; this is the missing primary-entity type and the spine every other People system reads from. Memory (per-person history) is folded in as its event-sourcing substrate — the same per-entity audit-trail the SO already applies to every primary entity — turning ad-hoc contact recall into a queryable, decayable asset. |
| **Tie-Strength & Cadence Engine** | Relationship maintenance as a portfolio — last-contact recency, target cadence per person, decay/health scoring, and surfacing who is overdue for a reach-out. | Dex / Clay nudges; Monica reminders; Superhuman follow-ups | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Directly parallels the SO's Dinacharya cadence model and Finance leverage-velocity spine: relationships decay without maintenance. A heartbeat/health score per tie (mirroring the Metrics card-heartbeat and account-health JTBD-completion patterns) makes neglect visible and converts 'I should reconnect' into a scheduled, gamified ritual. Distinct from the CRM registry: it is the derived health/decay layer on top of the roster, not the roster itself. |
| **Communications Hub (Unified Inbox)** | Inbound/outbound message flow across email, DMs, and messaging apps — triage, threads-per-person, response debt/SLAs, and follow-up tracking surfaced as relationship signal. | Superhuman / Shortwave / Spark; Texts.com unified inbox | 🟡 embed | ❌ not-built | Communication is the I/O layer of every relationship, but an email/IMAP/DM client is enormous and commoditized — the leverage is integration, not reinvention. Embed existing clients (Gmail/Calendar connectors exist in the harness) and surface only the relationship-relevant rollup — last-contact timestamps, open threads, response debt — into the CRM and Cadence engine. Nothing is built in the SO dashboard today; the value is the signal extraction, not the client. |
| **Household & Family Operations** | The closest-tie cluster run as an operating unit — family members, shared commitments and recurring care responsibilities, household/chore coordination, and high-weight dates (birthdays, anniversaries) that drive obligations. | Cozi / Maple / FamilyWall; Apple Family Sharing | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Family is a distinct, high-weight cluster carrying coordination and obligation machinery a generic CRM mis-models. As a specialized operations sub-registry it captures non-negotiable bonds and recurring care work, and feeds date-driven reminders into Dinacharya's daily ritual and Roadmap's task boards — the SO's existing scheduling surfaces. The one People sub-registry that earns system status because it is operations, not just a contact segment. |
| **Introductions & Network-Activation Engine** | Graph path-finding and brokering — second-degree reach, who can connect you to whom, and the log of warm intros made and received. | Boardy / Lunchclub; LinkedIn 'who can introduce me' | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | The defensible value of a relationship graph is path-finding: the ability to broker and to find a route to anyone. This repurposes the Network engine's existing BFS hop-expansion and impact-ripple traversal onto people, turning a static contact list into an actionable activation surface — the People analog of Finance's opportunity pipeline. Scoped to traversal/brokering; the give/get accounting lives in the Reciprocity Ledger. |
| **Commitments & Reciprocity Ledger** | The give/get accounting across the network — promises made and received, favors and debts, asks and offers in flight, and the running reciprocity balance per person and in aggregate. | Monica 'debts' / favor-tracking; a personal social-capital ledger | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Reciprocity is social capital, and capital needs a ledger. This is the People analog of Finance's portfolio/ledger spine — accounts-payable/receivable for relationships — and it consolidates the asks/offers and 'reciprocity balance' fragments that were previously scattered across an advisory-board lens and the intro engine. It converts diffuse goodwill into a tracked, settle-able balance that the Cadence and Activation systems can act on. |
| **Personal Team & Delegation** | The humans you pay or delegate to — assistants, contractors, service providers, vendors-as-people — with responsibilities owned, work in flight, and what each buys back in time. | a personal ops/EA tracker; Vendr / vendor-management for individuals | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Running life as an org means convening a team, not just a social circle. This is People-as-labor — the delegation resource — distinct from relationships and the direct counterpart to Finance's capital deployment: who you pay to buy back time. It routes work off your plate into Roadmap task boards with an owner, and is the People system most directly coupled to the SO's time-leverage thesis. |
| **Reputation & Public Presence** | How the superorganism is perceived externally — public profiles, mentions, audience/following, testimonials, and standing across platforms. | Brand24 / Mention; Linktree + social analytics | 🟡 embed | ❌ not-built | Reputation is the Trust-facing projection of People — what the network believes about you in aggregate. Listening/analytics is best embedded from existing tools, with only the rollup (follower counts, sentiment, notable mentions) flowing into Metrics as life-stat datasets — consistent with the SO's dataset/heartbeat Metrics architecture rather than a bespoke build. |

## Things (6)

| System | Manages | Analog | Verdict | State | Strategic fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Experience & Culture Catalog** | The library of consumed/collected cultural works — films, anime, albums, dishes, art — with rank, status, and curated juxtaposition | Letterboxd / Goodreads / Discogs / Beli | 🟢 build | ✅ built | Taste is a compounding personal asset; a ranked, curated catalog turns ephemeral consumption into a durable evaluation engine and feeds the Skill Tree (palate/aesthetic skills) and Network. | **Asset & Equipment Registry** | The single registry of owned durable things of value — across every class (real estate, electronics/devices, vehicles, furniture, valuables) — with location, condition, specs/serials, lifecycle & replacement, and the records that prove ownership (receipts, warranties, titles, policies) | Sortly / Snipe-IT (CMDB) + a Fixed-Assets ledger | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | The literal core of the Things bucket and its biggest gap: the SO tracks real estate only as a financial line (finance_account.real_estate), never the physical object graph — the Money lens and the Things lens are not interchangeable. Devices, vehicles, and ownership paperwork are facets here, not separate systems. |
| **Home & Environment / Spaces System** | The places I inhabit — rooms/zones, their setup and standards, improvement projects, and recurring maintenance — the physical substrate the daily ritual runs on | Notion home-OS / HomeZada / Houzz | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | Environment is the substrate the whole superorganism runs on; Dinacharya (ritual) and Roadmap (projects) both implicitly assume a maintained space. | **Consumables & Replenishment (Pantry/Supplies)** | Stock of depleting goods — food, supplements, household supplies — with on-hand quantity, reorder thresholds, and the consume/restock loop | AnyList / Bring! / Grocy | 🟡 embed | ❌ not-built | Dinacharya logs food behaviorally as ritual state, not as inventory; the 'do I have it / reorder it' loop is unmodeled. | **Acquisition & Disposition Pipeline (Wishlist → Own → Retire)** | The flow of things in and out — wishlist/research, purchase decisions, and end-of-life (sell/donate/discard) — with cost vs. value-of-keeping | Notion wishlist + resale (eBay / Facebook Marketplace) | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | The connective tissue between Exploration (want), Finance (afford/allocate), and the Asset Registry (own). Modeled as a pipeline it makes deliberate the otherwise-impulsive in/out flow of stuff and applies the Finance opportunity/leverage spine to physical purchases — a natural transpose of an existing SO pattern. |
| **Digital Asset & Media Estate** | The owned digital things — files & documents, photo/media libraries, domains, software licenses & keys, and purchased digital goods — with location, access, and lifecycle | Google Drive / Apple Photos + a license vault (e.g. Namecheap + a DAM) | 🟡 embed | ❌ not-built | The digital twin of the asset registry: a person running life as an org accumulates a large digital Things estate that is real, valuable, and today entirely unmodeled across scattered clouds. Strategic value is the inventory/registry lens (what do I own, where, can I find it, is the license current) — but the storage plumbing is a solved, commoditized problem, so embed the stores and add only the registry overlay rather than build a DAM. |

## Time (6)

|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Daily Ritual Engine (Dinacharya)** | The recurring daily cadence of body/mind practices (exercise, food, meditation, sleep) as a don't-break-the-chain game with streaks, spaced-repetition strength decay, a deep-work timer, plus a sandbox to trial new habits and graduate the ones that stick into the fixed daily set. | Streaks / Habitica / Routinery / Loop Habit Tracker | 🟢 build | ✅ built | The heartbeat of the superorganism — the recurring throughput that compounds. | **Roadmap & Goal Pipeline (Mission Control)** | Long-horizon personal goals decomposed into sub-projects → epics → sprints → tickets, moved through a status pipeline and grouped into Now/Next/Later phases, with a forward-and-historical phase-transition timeline giving the long-arc trajectory across all domains. | Linear / Jira / GitHub Projects / Roadmunk (timeline) | 🟢 build | ✅ built | The strategic-time layer: converts life intentions into structured, trackable, finite work and mirrors the org's product-roadmap machinery exactly. | **Theme & Horizon Cascade** | The OKR-style cascade of yearly themes → quarterly objectives → weekly priorities that gives every roadmap item and ritual a why, anchoring daily cadence to life mission. | Tability / Ahead / Cortex OKRs / Quarterly | 🟢 build | ⚪ stub | Without a horizon cascade the superorganism optimizes locally (today's streak) and drifts strategically. | **Reflection & Review Cadence** | The recurring weekly/monthly/quarterly review workflow — prompts, retrospectives, and recalibration that close the forecast→reality→recalibrate loop by re-aligning rituals, roadmap, and themes against what actually happened. | Sunsama (weekly review) / Reflect / Ahead reviews / Dayone (review prompts) | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | The Act/recalibrate loop of the time domain, distinct from the Cascade that sets direction. ## Trust (8)

| System | Manages | Analog | Verdict | State | Strategic fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Threat & Security Posture (SecMon)** | Personal attack-surface inventory, STRIDE threat model, risk register, live posture board (service accounts, RLS/rotation, dependency audits) AND the response half: incident log, recovery runbooks (lost device, account compromise, SIM-swap), and annual tabletops. | Vanta / Drata (posture) + a STRIDE canvas + a PagerDuty-style runbook wiki | 🟢 build | 🟡 partial | The console IS the organization, so its own security page is the highest-value target and must be first-party. | **Identity, Access & Secrets** | Canonical registry of every account, device, and SSO identity (2FA coverage, least-privilege, session/privileged-access reviews), the master credential vault, and the agent-authorization model — scoped, short-lived, revocable grants by which Claude Code and other agents prove access. | Okta + 1Password Watchtower (registry/2FA) + 1Password/Bitwarden (vault) + a scoped-token/OAuth broker for agents | 🟢 build | ⚪ stub | You can't secure, rotate, or off-board what you haven't enumerated; the registry is the join table the rest of Trust points at. | **Exposure & Breach Defense** | Data-minimization and breach posture in one register: credit freezes at all three bureaus, IRS PIN, SSN/passport exposure, dark-web/HIBP monitoring, plus email aliases per service, phone compartmentalization, VPN/DNS filtering, and recurring data-broker opt-outs. | Aura / DeleteMe / Optery (broker + identity monitoring) + Have I Been Pwned + SimpleLogin/Hide-My-Email + NextDNS | 🟡 embed | ❌ not-built | External services own the data, remediation, and removal tooling; SO's job is to aggregate their alerts and track freeze/PIN/exposure and re-opt-out cadence in one register so nothing silently lapses. | **Health & Body Integrity** | The body as the org's irreplaceable substrate: medical records, labs/biomarkers, medications, providers, advance directives, and the physical-training/sleep/nutrition signals that gate readiness. | Apple Health / Epic MyChart (records) + a personal biomarker tracker | 🟢 build | 🟡 partial | Trust in the SO's continuity ultimately rests on the body. | **Estate & Continuity Planning** | Will, healthcare directive, power of attorney, beneficiary designations, digital-estate access, crypto-key handoff, password-manager emergency access, and a letter of instruction for the executor. | Trust & Will / Everplans (digital estate vault) | 🟢 build | ❌ not-built | The infinite-game framing made literal: continuity of the organization beyond its founder. | **Commitments & Integrity Ledger** | Promises made and kept across relationships — agreements, obligations, follow-through, and reciprocity — making personal reliability legible and auditable. | A relationship CRM (Clay / Monica) with a commitments/follow-up layer | 🟢 build | ⚪ stub | Trust as social capital: an org earns it by keeping commitments.
