# REFERENCE — AI Vending Machines (Superorganism)

Superorganism's slice of the vending standard, organized by app archetype — the inward, self-facing machines.

## The standard — 8 traits

1. **Cheap, on-hand input** · 2. **One bounded outcome** (via the machine's mode) · 3. **No per-unit incumbent for the small buyer** · 4. **Low liability** · 5. **Horizontal** · 6. **Reasoning over messy input is the work** · 7. **High leverage** · 8. **A real moat** — a domain-specific structure/trust layer, not raw generation.

## Console (1)
- **GrowReport** — *decode / differential moat* — crop-symptom photos (+ crop type) → a scouting action sheet: ranked causes with cited evidence, severity, a look-alike differential, and a sequenced IPM plan with a re-scout date
  - **Moat:** a symptom→cause differential diagnostic key + a crop-specific IPM action-sequence library

## Sandbox (7)

**TimeWarpKiln** — *Dreamtime Yield (+ Emergence)*
- a credit + a seed (worldlet + goal stack), or a design under conditions → the max harvestable stock after N weeks of compressed world-time (with the conversion rate it ran at), or the verdict on whether the design hit the target — one kiln, two reads; pay-per-fork to test a design
- *moat:* a time-warp speed governor with determinism-equivalence (the conversion-rate dial is the product) · *buyer:* worldbuilders maturing a seed; anyone testing a design's outcome

**LegendsForge** — *Accreted History*
- a credit + a founding seed + a run length → a readable saga of a once-only history (foundings, feuds, golden ages, ruins) threaded from the event log
- *moat:* append-only causal sediment + seeded-RNG ('could never run twice') · *buyer:* GMs, fiction/lore writers

**RuinsCartographer** — *Accreted History*
- a credit + a world-state/seed + a dig depth → a replayable, time-scrubbable chronicle of how the present came to be, with a provenance-tagged causal map
- *moat:* deterministic replay + the intervention-provenance ledger (every 'because' is a provable edge) · *buyer:* studios/researchers/GMs documenting a world's origin

**SocietyFork** — *Emergent Order & Society (+ Emergence)*
- a credit + a society (running or seed) → fork it: **keep one** (a private instance to run and inhabit) or **compare N** (parallel forks under a divergence, returned side-by-side) — one fork engine; own a copy or audition the alternates
- *moat:* society-state capture + fork semantics + a divergence/comparison layer · *buyer:* strategists comparing outcomes; anyone wanting their own society to keep

**VivariumNudge** — *Stewardship Leverage*
- a credit + an intervention (a message/rule/agent/incentive) → injected into a *running* vivarium, propagated to a world-scale before/after consequence
- *moat:* the live-intervention engine + autonomous amplification + off-the-wheel honest read · *buyer:* people running/watching a persistent agent world

**AgentDraft** — *Liveness & Inhabited Presence*
- a credit *per agent* → a specific agent (persona, memory, skills, relationships) instantiated into your world as a persistent inhabitant
- *moat:* an agent catalog + persistent-identity transfer — *agents-as-collectibles* · *buyer:* people populating their own society; collectible-agent buyers

**CapsuleForge** — *Accreted History*
- a credit + a world/seed/history-slice → a beautiful, collectible generative story / capsule / animated comic, canon-coherent within the worldbuilding
- *moat:* a world-canon consistency engine + a polished collectible-render pipeline · *buyer:* collectors, fans, worldbuilders
