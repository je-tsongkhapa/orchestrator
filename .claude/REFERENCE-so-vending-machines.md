# REFERENCE — AI Vending Machines (Superorganism)

Superorganism's slice of the vending standard (demand-side sibling to `REFERENCE-so-business-systems.md`) — the inward, self-facing machines. Organized by app archetype; grows as each archetype is sorted by org.

## The standard — 8 traits

1. **Cheap, on-hand input** · 2. **One bounded outcome** (via the machine's mode) · 3. **No per-unit incumbent for the small buyer** · 4. **Low liability** · 5. **Horizontal** · 6. **Reasoning over messy input is the work** · 7. **High leverage** · 8. **A real moat** — a domain-specific structure/trust layer, not raw generation (3 moat-types: decode-table · criteria-matrix · closure-tie-out).

## Console
- **GrowReport** — *decode / differential moat* — crop-symptom photos (+ crop type) → a scouting action sheet: ranked causes with cited evidence, severity, a look-alike differential, and a sequenced IPM plan with a re-scout date
  - **Buyer:** Small farms, vineyards, orchardists, nurseries, golf/turf managers, and serious growers without an on-call agronomist. Pays $20-75 per scouting event versus a $150-300 crop-consultant visit they can't afford per incident.
  - **Moat:** a symptom→cause differential diagnostic key + a crop-specific IPM action-sequence library
